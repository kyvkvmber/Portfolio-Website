The Best Movies According to Paul

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Martian

A movie about an Astronaut stranded in Mars. This movie greatly depicts on what is like for humans to live in Mars.

Things I like about the movie:

. It is a space movie
. Very imaginative
. Showed the technicalities for space flights

Interstellar

A movie about a group of Astronauts visiting other planets to establish as the new home for humans. This movie accurately represented the struggles of finding a new planet to live in.

Things I like about the movie:

. It is a space movie
. Very imaginative
. It did a good job of depicting various theories from well-reknowned Physicists . The blackhole

Aliens

A movie about an extra-terrestrial lifeform threating the human species. This movie gives the real sense of dread and hopelessness in the face of an extra-terristrial lifeform.

Things I like about the movie:

. It is a space movie
. Very imaginative
. The plot was really interesting and did a good job of showing the hopelessness of humans in the face of an extra-terrestrial lifeform . The world-building is otherworldly.
